Lava Cake Recipe

  • Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit.
  • Spray four custard cups with baking spray and place on a baking sheet.
  • Microwave bittersweet chocolate, semisweet chocolate and butter in a bold for one minute.
  • Whisk until the chocolate in the bowl is well melted.
  • Stir in the sugar.
  • Mix in the stirred egg yolks.
  • Add some vanilla.
  • Put the mixture into the custard cups.
  • Bake the custard cakes until the sides of the lava cake are firm and the center is soft. This will probably take 13 minutes but the time may vary.
  • After this serve the lava cake with a nice scoop of vanilla bean ice cream.

Ingredients to Recipe

  • Baking spray
  • Custard cups
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 ounces of semi sweet chocolate
  • 1 1/4 cups of powdered
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 3 eggs yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • Vanilla ice cream
